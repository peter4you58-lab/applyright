Questions

Before you send
anything

Will you put things on my CV that aren't true?

No. Everything comes from what you give us and stays true to your actual experience. We change emphasis, wording and ordering to match the job description — never the facts. If a role needs something you don't have, we tell you rather than inventing it.

Do you guarantee interviews?

No — and be careful with anyone who does. What's guaranteed is a properly tailored application, submitted to the role you asked for, with a logged row proving it. The interview is between you and the employer.

How do I know the applications were really sent?

Two ways. Your sheet has a row for every one, with the exact CV and cover letter we used. And most employers send their own automatic confirmation straight to your inbox — so you can verify us against a source we don't control.

Do you apply to roles I wouldn't want?

Only what matches the criteria you set — titles, industries, locations, salary floor, and your avoid list. Anything borderline, we ask first.

Will employers know someone else applied for me?

The application is submitted from your details and in your name, with your permission — this is the same arrangement as a recruiter acting on your behalf. Everything we write, you can read before or after it goes out.

How do I pay from the UK, US or Canada?

Card, PayPal or bank transfer, invoiced in USD. Bundles are paid in advance; singles are billed for what's been submitted. No card is stored and there is no recurring charge.

Can I stop?

Any time, with no notice. Unused bundle credits don't expire, so they'll be waiting if you come back.
